1. Object Mode
Be sure that your blender is in "Object Mode".
If you're not in "Object Mode", go to bottom menu and select "Object Mode" after "Object" button menu

2. Selection
Select your 3d object with the RMB
Three colored arrows will appear at the center of the 3d object

3. Move in 3 axis
Move with press the LMB (without releas it) on 1 of 3 colored arrow.
Release your LMB when you finish to move your object
